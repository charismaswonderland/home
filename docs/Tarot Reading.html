Tarot Reading

Tarot has been around for a long time... although there's a lot of speculation surrounding its origins, ther is a lot of information that's universally agreed upon. It can be used to answer questions about life, guide you in times of uncertainty, and used for just plain fun.

The two types of decks I use for my readings are described in the details below.

Standard Tarot Deck

For this type of reading, I use a standard Rider-Waite-Smith Tarot deck. My preferred deck is the original design from Englad in 1910.

I have a variety of these decks.

Oracle Deck

If my standard Tarot decks aren't something you're comfortable with, I have a variety of gentler decks to choose from.

My fiance prefers the Mermaids and Dolphins deck!
